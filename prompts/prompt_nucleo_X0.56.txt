DEFINICIONES INAMOVIBLES:
  FCF = CFO − "Purchases of property and equipment" (Cash Flow Statement EDGAR)
  Cash real = CashAndCashEquivalents + ShortTermInvestments (NUNCA solo Cash)
  Market Cap = Precio × todas las clases de acciones (A+B+C+dilutivas)
  Deuda Total = Long-term debt + Current portion + convertibles

  TIER 2: StockAnalysis.com/stocks/[TICKER]/
  TIER 3: TradingView Scanner API
  TIER 4: Macrotrends · Yahoo Finance · web_search directo

  PEG · P/E Forward · EPS Proj · Margen Bruto · ROIC · Beta · D/E · Revenue YoY
